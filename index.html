CARE AND HEALTH FOR
TOUR CACTUS

  • In cold times, add water once a month and during the summer do it when the soil is very dry.

  • Have good drainage so that the cactus does not accumlate water.

  • Place your cactus or succulant in a lacation with indirect light.

  • Do not water more than necessary, as it can accumlate too mutch water and the plant will rot.

  • Do not expose to high temperatures, as succulant plants are not fans of extremes.
